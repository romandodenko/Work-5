Asteroids: Addictive arcade game

The goal of the game is to get as many points as possible by shooting the asteroids while avoiding the debris. The player controls an arrow-shaped spaceship that can turn left and right, as well as move and shoot, but only forward. When moving, momentum is not preserved: if you do not turn on the engine, the ship will gradually stop. The player can also use a hyperspace drive - this causes the ship to disappear and then reappear at a random location on the screen, at the risk of being destroyed due to the appearance of an asteroid in place.

Each level begins with the appearance of several asteroids drifting at random points on the screen. The edges of the screen are wrapped towards each other, for example, an asteroid that goes beyond the top edge of the screen appears at the bottom and continues to move in the same direction. When the player hits an asteroid, it breaks into pieces that are smaller but move faster.
